About Me

I received the B.S. degree in communication engineering from Wuhan University of Technology, Wuhan, China, in 2015, with Prof. KeWen Liu. I then obtained the M.S. degree in communication and information systems engineering from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Chengdu, China, in 2018, with Prof. Xuesong Tan. I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Department of Electronic and Electrical Engineering, University College London, London, U.K, with Prof. Miguel Rodrigues.
